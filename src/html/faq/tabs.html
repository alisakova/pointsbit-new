What is Crypto?

Crypto is a form of digital currency, created and held electronically. It is the first decentralized peer-to-peer payment network that is powered by its users without having a central authority or middlemen involved.

How long does it take for Pointsbit to send my Crypto once I’ve paid?

Pointsbit sends Crypto immediately after the client’s payment details have been confirmed and authenticated. Delays might occur as a result of purchasing outside of business hours (GMT) or regardless of hours of operation.

What methods of payment does Pointsbit offer?

At Pointsbit, you can purchase Crypto with your choice of credit/debit card or by using an alternative payment method.

What if I want to buy more/less coins than the amounts offered?

If you would like to buy different amounts, please use the “Slider” (the price calculator) located at the bottom of the homepage or the input for entering the desired sum.
